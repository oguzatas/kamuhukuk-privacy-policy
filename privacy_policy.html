Kamuhukuk Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-11-13
Last Updated: 2025-07-11

🔍 Information Collection and Use

Kamuhukuk does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names or emails.

However, it may automatically collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • Approximate location
  • Device specifications (OS, language)
  • Usage statistics
  • Anonymous analytics via third-party services

This data helps improve performance and manage in-app subscriptions.

🌐 Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat
  • Google Play Services

📍 Location Data

Only approximate location data is used, primarily for content relevance. No GPS-level tracking is performed.

👦 Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it is immediately deleted.

🔒 Data Security

Kamuhukuk applies standard security practices such as:

  • Encrypted HTTPS communication
  • Anonymized and aggregated data storage
  • Access control and routine audits

🚫 Opt-Out Rights

You may opt out at any time by uninstalling the application.

🗂️ Data Retention

Minimal data is stored only as long as necessary, and always anonymized.

🔁 Changes

We may revise this policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
